FOOD & NUTRITION

Food and Nutrition is an interactive, informative class where students hone various cooking skills in the kitchen. Not only do students get a chance to experiment in the kitchen, they also learn about everything there is to know about food -- the importance of different nutrients, how to keep a balanced diet, and many more.

MEET THE TEACHER

Mr. Febrian Ralahalu is an experienced, and knowledgeable culinary mentor who has been assisting our students in their culinary ventures for many years. He has much knowledge about food, including cooking skills, safety procedures in the kitchen and the importance of nutrients as he has worked in a professional kitchen before coming to Springfield to share his knowledge and skills with our students.
