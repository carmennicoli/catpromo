WHY CHOOSE F&N?

Some people might say that Food and Nutrition class is not useful because you can learn to cook anywhere, anytime. However, Food and Nutrition is much more than that. You are able to nurture your cooking skills and express your creativity in the form of food. Students will be able to gain confidence and try new things without the fear of committing an error or failing. It prepares you to take your first step to adulthood and enhances your ability to be independent. This class is great for everyone, whether you are already skilled or merely a beginner.

WHAT STUDENTS LEARN

In Food and Nutrition class, students acquire a lot of different skills, ranging from how to properly handle a knife to the importance of why different nutrients are needed for a balanced diet and a healthy lifestyle. Not only that, students are requiring an essential life skill needed to advance into adulthood and achieve independence.

EQUIPMENT AND FACILITIES

The Food and Nutrition kitchen is where the magic happens. Filled with top-notch equipments such as non-stick pans, complete sets of high-quality knives, measuring tools, and cooking utensils, the students are able to learn and experience how to utilise different tools to get the best results. With large sinks from left to right and large countertops, students are able to work in a sanitary environment without any worries.
