SWEET CORN CHIFFON CAKE

INGREDIENTS

- 6 egg yolks

- 140 gr flour (kunci biru)

- 1 tbsp corn flour

- ¾ tsp baking powder

- 100 gr canned sweet corn

- 50 ml fresh milk

- 1 tsp pandan extract

- 60 ml corn oil

- 50 gr cheddar cheese

- 7 egg whites

- 50 gr sugar

- ¾ tsp cream of tartar


DIRECTIONS

1. Process sweet corn and fresh milk in food processor until smooth.

2. Grate the cheese.

3. Beat egg yolks with a balloon whisk and mix in 50 gr sugar. Add the blended sweet corn and oil. Mix well.

4. Sift in flour, corn flour and baking powder. Mix well.

5. Add the cheese.

6. In another mixing bowl, beat egg whites and cream of tartar with electric mixer (speed no.1 only) until bubbly. Then gradually add the 50 gr sugar and keep beating until stiff peak formed.

7. Add the meringue into egg yolk mixture in 3 batches. Mix well after each addition.

8. Pour the cake batter into the cake mold, top with grated cheese and bake in 180 C preheated oven for 50 minutes.
